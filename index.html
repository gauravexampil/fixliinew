Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to all your questions about Fixlii's services, bookings, pricing, and more.

1. What types of services does Fixlii offer?

Fixlii provides a wide range of home services, including electrical repairs, plumbing, cleaning, appliance repair, beauty & wellness, cook services, driver services, painting, home improvement, and home tutor services.

2. How do I book a service on Fixlii?

You can book a service by visiting our website or mobile app, selecting the required service, choosing a convenient time slot, and confirming the booking.

3. Are the professionals verified and trained?

Yes, all Fixlii professionals undergo background verification and are trained to ensure high-quality service and customer satisfaction.

4. How is the pricing determined?

Our pricing is transparent and competitive. You’ll be informed of the service cost before booking, ensuring no hidden charges or surprises.
